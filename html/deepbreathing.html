Deep Breathing Exercise

Calm your mind with controlled breathing 🌱

Practice Deep Breathing

Breathe

Follow the circle:
Inhale when it grows, Exhale when it becomes smaller.

📝 Steps to Deep Breathing:

  1. Find a comfortable position - Sit or lie down in a relaxed posture
  2. Close your eyes - Focus on your breathing and block out distractions
  3. Inhale slowly - Breathe in through your nose for 4 counts
  4. Hold your breath - Hold for 4-6 counts to let oxygen circulate
  5. Exhale slowly - Breathe out through your mouth for 4 counts
  6. Repeat 5-10 times - Continue this cycle for optimal calming effects

Benefits of Deep Breathing

😌 Stress Relief

Reduces cortisol levels and anxiety instantly.

❤️ Heart Health

Lowers blood pressure and improves circulation.

🧠 Mental Clarity

Increases oxygen to brain for better focus.

Daily Deep Breathing Routine

🌅 Morning: Start with 5 minutes of deep breathing to energize.

⚡ During Stress: Use 2-3 minutes of deep breathing to calm down.

🌙 Evening: Practice 10 minutes before bed for better sleep.
